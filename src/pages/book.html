Overview

In Grace Retraces Her Day, young Grace embarks on a magical journey through her everyday world with her favorite toy, Caterpillar. When she misplaces her beloved friend, Grace must retrace her steps, learning that sometimes the most important things are found in unexpected places. This charming children's book explores themes of imagination, adventure, and the love of family.

Perfect for children ages 3-7, this picture book invites readers to dive into Grace’s world of make-believe, teaching them valuable life lessons along the way.

Story Highlights

Here are some memorable moments from Grace’s exciting day:

  • “Retrace your steps,” said Mom. So, Grace is making the rounds.
  • Up in her room, Grace whined and resisted when she discovered that Caterpillar was gone.
  • Grace couldn’t rest without her. “No, Mom, that wasn’t a yawn!”
  • By noon, Mom called Grace to change. Her wet bathing suit was getting itchy.
  • Then it was time for breakfast. Grace wanted chocolate. Dad said pancakes.
  • Grace sat with Caterpillar (and her two sisters). They compromised on what to make.

Why You’ll Love It

Grace Retraces Her Day is more than just a story of a lost toy—it's a celebration of childhood curiosity and the power of imagination. Through playful storytelling and vibrant illustrations, this heartwarming book helps children understand problem-solving, compromise, and the joy of family.

About the Author

CHERYL LOCK has known since the third grade when she read Robert C. O’Brien’s Mrs. Frisby and the Rats of NIMH that writing — and writing children’s books, more specifically — would be in her future. Eventually, she moved to Manhattan to follow her writing dreams, working for newspapers, magazines, and websites. Although this is her first children’s book, her work has been published in The New York Times, The Washington Post, Forbes, Parents, and loads of other places. She lives in Colorado with her husband and three daughters.
