The Party in the Old Brown Barn

By Cheryl Lock

FORMAT: Hardcover, Paperback, eBook

Buy on Amazon Buy on Barnes & Noble Buy signed copy from author

"When the old brown barn at the end of the street is torn down to make way for something new, the animals that live there have a choice. They can focus on feeling sad or make the most of the time they have."

In this delightfully hand-illustrated picture book, kids learn a valuable lesson about making the most of the situations we're given. Children will also learn about the different habitats for the animals that live in the old brown bar at the end of the street. They'll discover some of the different ways that we can live harmoniously with the world around us, and what we can do to help preserve the nature that is all around us.

Grace Retraces Her Day

By Cheryl Lock

FORMAT: Hardcover, Paperback, eBook

Buy on Amazon Buy on Barnes & Noble Buy on Books-A-MillionBuy signed copy from author

"Retrace your steps,” said Mom.
So, Grace is making the rounds.

When Grace misplaces her beloved toy caterpillar, she brings us along on a magical journey through her day to find it. As we walk through Grace's world, both she and the reader discover that sometimes the most important things are found in the most unexpected places.

Perfect for young children of all ages, this picture book invites readers to dive into Grace’s world of make-believe, teaching them valuable life lessons of imagination, adventure and play along the way.

Why You’ll Love It

Grace Retraces Her Day is more than just a story about a lost toy. It's a celebration of childhood curiosity and the power of imagination. Through playful storytelling and vibrant illustrations, this heartwarming book helps children understand problem-solving, compromise, and the joy of family. The book was a finalist in the American Writing Awards Children's Book International contest in the "adventure" category, and the 2025 Literary Global Children's Book Awards in the "realistic fiction" category.

Introducing Book & Craft Birthday Parties!

Looking for a unique and engaging way to celebrate your child’s birthday?

The Financial Caregiver Kit

By Cheryl Lock

FORMAT: eBook

Over 60% of family caregivers help with paying bills, managing bank accounts, or handling insurance paperwork — often under pressure.

The Financial Caregiver Kit is a practical guide designed specifically for adult children and family members who find themselves managing finances for aging parents or loved ones.

I created this guide, with insights from former LegalZoom executives, to combine real-world caregiving experience with deep financial and legal expertise.

This isn't just another financial planning book — it's a compassionate companion that understands the unique decisions, emotions, and responsibilities you face as a financial caregiver. Inside the book you'll find:

  • Templates and checklists
  • Financial + legal basics in plain english
  • Scripts for hard conversations

About the Author

CHERYL LOCK has known since the third grade when she read Robert C. O’Brien’s Mrs. Frisby and the Rats of NIMH that writing — and writing children’s books, more specifically — would be in her future. Eventually, she moved to Manhattan to follow her writing dreams, working for newspapers, magazines, and websites. Her work has been published in The New York Times, The Washington Post, Forbes, Parents, and loads of other places. She lives in Colorado with her husband and three daughters.
